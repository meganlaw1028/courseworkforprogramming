    Hong Kong: Here and Beyond

    The visual culture of our city from the 1960s to now. Told through individual and collective histories, Hong Kong: Here and Beyond captures the city’s transformation from the post-war decades to the present day. Home to ground-breaking artists, architects, designers, and filmmakers, Hong Kong is a site of intense creative ferment and social and cultural transformation.
